# Payment Gateway

If you're looking for a means of easily transacting payments on the Terra Blockchain Ecosystem, then look no further. We have recently launched a payment gateway that makes it extremely easy to offer a means of crypto payments for all your customers.

Terra Payments is compatible on any Platform and any Ecosystem. Simply select the Payment SDK that fits your platform, configure your business wallet, and start receiving [LUNC](https://coinmarketcap.com/currencies/terra-luna/) and [LUNA](https://coinmarketcap.com/currencies/terra-luna-v2/) from your customers.
